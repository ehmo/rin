# Introduction

Rin is a contact-graph social product designed around verified private contact channels, controlled sharing, and relationship quality.

## What This Guide Covers
- Product intent and user value.
- End-to-end system architecture and operations.
- iOS and App Store delivery requirements.
- Marketing, monetization, and long-term roadmap.

## Core Constraints
- iOS-first client.
- Go/Fiber backend.
- NATS for queueing/events.
- PostgreSQL first, with guardrails for scale-up.
- OSS-first infrastructure strategy.
