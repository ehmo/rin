Rin Logo V5 — 12 SVG Concepts

Synthesis of V1–V4 learnings. One bold symbol, one ownable structural move.
Optimized for social-category home screen presence. No letterforms, no chat bubbles, no generic network meshes.

01

Ripple Break

Two concentric thick rings sharing one aligned break/gap. Signal escaping outward.

02

Split Disc

Solid disc split by a vertical gap with offset halves. Connection through separation.

03

Focal Ring

Thick ring with a centered solid dot. Focus, core insight, a lens finding signal.

04

Wedge View

Solid circle with a triangular wedge removed. Selective vision into the network.

05

Linked Circles

Two overlapping thick-stroke circles. Trusted connection between two entities.

06

Horizon Disc

Solid circle bisected by a horizontal gap. Analysis, clarity through division.

07

Corner Key

Rounded square with one corner replaced by a concave cut. Selective access and trust.

08

Signal Arc

Thick 270-degree arc with a node at one terminal. Reach and direction.

09

Offset Pair

Two different-sized solid circles in diagonal offset. Hierarchy and relationship.

10

Split Monolith

Tall rounded rectangle split by a horizontal gap. Bridge between two solid forms.

11

Ring Terminal

Three-quarter ring with asymmetric terminals — flat cut vs rounded bulb.

12

Nested Squares

Two concentric rounded squares with one shared notch. Layers with one access point.
